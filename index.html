Professional, Ceramic Coatings


Brought to you by, Vivid Pro Auto Spa. Your local Professional, Mobile Detailer.

Hydrophobic Properties

Ceramic coatings provide exceptional hydrophobic benefits by creating a durable, nano-ceramic layer on the vehicle's surface that repels water, dirt, and contaminants. This layer minimizes water spots and helps prevent grime from adhering, making cleaning significantly easier.

Superior Protection

They protect against harmful elements like bird droppings, acid rain, UV rays, and road salts, which can damage paint.

Enhanced Appearance

Ceramic coatings enhance a vehicle's appearance by adding a deep, glossy finish and preserving the paint's color and vibrancy over time.
